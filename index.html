Imperial College London is a world-class research university located in London, UK, one of the core organizations of British universities, one of the four Commonwealth universities in BRICS. Imperial College London has outstanding performance in engineering, science, medicine, business and other fields, and is one of the world's top universities. Imperial College's motto is "Scientia imperii decus et tutamen", It means "science is the glory and protection of empires". The college currently offers many world-leading professional courses and gathers top scholars and students from all over the world to work together on the promotion of scientific research and education.
